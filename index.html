Football Expected Points Calculator

A Calculator for Fourth Down Options

So you're at fourth down, and you are running out of options, and you do not have much yards to go left. What option would be the best? To go for it? To punt? What about a field goal? Our graph answers all those questions and more.

All data analysis is current for data taken from Divison 3 athletics up to year 2016. Since electronic devices are not allowed in an active football game, other ways are highly recommend when trying to this graph in a game.

Q: What exactly is shown in this graph?

A: Expected points for three different scenarios for 4th down plays are shown in this graph.

Features of D3 Expected Points Calculator

Calculated and Accessed Instantly

No long load times, database is ready before you are ready to look at it. Updated every year as data is automatically collected from Division 3 athletics archival logs.

Data Automatically Graphed

For each valid yard line and distance value in the fourth down, we are calculating the expected points for the drive, punt, and field goal through a heavily tested formula.
